Crispy Honey Chilli Chicken

Description

Chinese takeaway style Honey Chilli Chicken recipe made with coated crispy fried chicken tossed with spicy, sweet, honey sauce. Perfect as an appetizer and made with simple cupboard-friendly ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 350g chicken breast
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 cup corn starch
  • 2 tsp oil
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 tbsp garlic

Marinate for Chicken

  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

Chilli Sauce

  • 3 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes

Garnish

  • 1 red chilli pepper cut thin slices
  • 2 spring onions cut thin slices

Steps

  1. First, cut the chicken breast into thin strips. Place the chicken strips in a bowl, marinade with soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic paste, sugar, and pepper for 10-15 minutes. Mix all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl and set it aside.
  2. Next, add egg white into the chicken and mix it well. Then add the corn starch and coat each chicken piece. ( Add more corn starch if needed. ) Toss the excess flour out before frying.
  3. Deep fry the coated chicken pieces with pre-heated high-heat oil, 350° F / 176° C for 1-2 minutes until light brown. Remove from oil and set it aside for 4-5 minutes. Then place the fried chicken back in hot oil and fry it again for another 2-3 minutes or until crispy golden. Remove from oil and place it over the wire rack.
  4. Heat the wok/pan into medium-low heat, drizzle 2 tsp of oil and saute the chopped garlic for few seconds. Then pour the sauce mixture in and stir for few seconds, place the fried chicken strips back in the wok.
  5. Combine the sauce and chicken pieces well. Turn off the heat and sprinkle fresh chilli slices and spring onions.
  6. Transfer to serving plate and serve immediately.
  7. You can serve on its own or pair it with plain rice, egg fried rice or stir fry noodles.
