Thai Wings

Description

Crispy fried chicken wings glazed with flavourful Thai-style spicy sticky sauce. These Spicy Thai wings are highly addictive wing recipes of all time. So simple and easy to make at home with basic everyday ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken wings
  • 2 cups potato starch
  • Oil for frying
  • 3 garlic cloves

Marinate for Chicken

  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Wing Sauce

  • 3 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp soy sauce

Garnish

  • 2-3 slices kaffir lime leaves

Steps

  1. Put chicken wings in a large bowl, add all the ingredients from the marinate list. Mix all the ingredients and rub it to chicken wings well. Set aside and marinate at least 30 mins.
  2. Roughly chop the garlic.
  3. Mix all ingredients from the wing sauce list in a bowl and set aside.
  4. In a large tray pour the corn/potato flour in. Coat each of the chicken wings well with the flour. Toss the excess flour out before frying.
  5. Heat the oil into high heat about 350° F / 176° C. Add the coated wings and deep fry for 8-10 mins till crispy golden brown. Remove from oil and transfer to a cooling rack or paper towel.
  6. Heat a pan, drizzle 2 tsp of oil, add the garlic and stir for 1-2 mins till the nice garlic smell comes out, then add the wing sauce mix. Lower the heat and stir for a few seconds.
  7. Turn off the heat and add the fried wings in the pan. Stir and combine the wings and sauce together well.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate and garnish with kaffir lime leaves strips.
  9. Super delicious Thai Wings are ready! Enjoy!
